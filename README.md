
# 🔨 **Context Forge**  
### *Craft, refine, and optimize AI context with clarity and control*

**Context Forge** is your all‑in‑one workspace for building high‑quality AI context.  
Use it instantly at **[https://iggym.github.io/context-forge](https://iggym.github.io/context-forge)** or explore the source at **[https://github.com/iggym/context-forge](https://github.com/iggym/context-forge)**.

It’s designed to feel warm, intuitive, and empowering — a creative workshop where humans and agents shape context with precision.

---

## 🌟 **What Context Forge Does (Simple, Friendly Explanation)**

Each feature helps you *work smarter with AI* — no technical background required.

- **Dashboard** — Your home base showing recent work, saved items, and tools.
- **Context Extractor** — Paste text and instantly get the important parts pulled out for you.
- **Context Builder** — Combine pieces of information like building blocks to form a strong prompt.
- **Context Optimizer** — Cleans up your context so it’s shorter, clearer, and more effective.
- **Memory Manager** — Save reusable knowledge so you don’t have to repeat yourself.
- **Context Evaluator** — Checks how good your context is before you send it to an AI.
- **Playground** — Try out prompts and contexts in a fun, interactive space.
- **Local Persistence** — Everything stays on your device. Nothing is uploaded.

---

## 🧠 **Technical Feature Overview (For Engineers & Power Users)**

- **Dashboard** — State‑driven aggregation of persisted artifacts, memory entries, and recent operations.
- **Context Extractor** — Hybrid deterministic + LLM‑assisted extraction of entities, tasks, constraints, and domain signals.
- **Context Builder** — Modular composition engine supporting hierarchical assembly, templating, and multi‑segment orchestration.
- **Context Optimizer** — Performs compression, deduplication, semantic clustering, and relevance scoring.
- **Memory Manager** — Structured long‑term memory with tagging, embedding‑based retrieval, and local‑only persistence.
- **Context Evaluator** — Heuristic + LLM scoring across clarity, completeness, redundancy, and task alignment.
- **Playground** — Lightweight inference sandbox with pluggable model backends and real‑time context injection.
- **Local Persistence** — IndexedDB‑backed storage with structured serialization for zero‑backend operation.

---

## 🎨 **Design Philosophy**

- **Craftsmanship** — Tools should feel like well‑made instruments, not black boxes.  
- **Clarity** — You should always understand what your context contains and why.  
- **Agency** — You stay in control; the system helps you shape, not guess.  
- **Local‑First** — Privacy and speed matter, so everything stays on your device.
